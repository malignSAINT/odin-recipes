Ingredients

  • Broth
  • sliced peppers
  • fresh garlic
  • Gochujang
  • Chili Crisp
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • Sambal Oelek
  • Sriracha Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Ramen Noodles
  • Shrimp
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Heat the broth
  2. Stir in the spicy ingredients
  3. simmer
  4. Stri in the noodles and shrimp cook for about 5 minutes.
  5. Serve your Spicy Ramen Noodles!
