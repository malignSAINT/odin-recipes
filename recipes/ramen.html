Ramen for the uncommon man!

Sometimes we all need a little pep in our step and with this amazing spicy ramen noodle dish you will be ready to take on the world!

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • sliced peppers
  • fresh garlic
  • Gochujang
  • Chili Crisp
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • Sambal Oelek
  • Sriracha Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Ramen Noodles
  • Shrimp
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Heat the broth
  2. Stir in the spicy ingredients
  3. simmer
  4. Stri in the noodles and shrimp cook for about 5 minutes.
  5. Serve your Spicy Ramen Noodles!
